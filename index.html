WRHA emergency wait times

Data obtained from WRHA's site. I'm polling the site at 5 minute intervals.

whra_wait_times.csv.

Field list

  1. Timestamp, in seconds since epoch, as reported on the WRHA's page (in GMT)
  2. Facility name
  3. Number of patients waiting
  4. Average Wait
  5. Longest non-urgent wait

You may see duplicate entries depending on when I poll vs when the site is updated. Use fields 1 and 2 for deduplication.

  • Oct 2: Fixed timestamps by adding 18000 to make them into GMT
